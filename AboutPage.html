About Us

Welcome to Fluffy Paws! We look forward to helping you with all of your pet's needs. Our friendly and knowledgeable team are ready to offer any guidance you may need to care for your animals! Our team has an array of personal pets so chances are that we will have the answers to your questions, if not, we are committed to finding them. Welcome and we look forward to meeting you! Store Team Leader, Nichole Lay is a firm believer in great nutrition for all of her animals and feeds Redford Naturals kibble and treats. Shift Leader, Sara has two dogs, two cats and a crested gecko. She feeds Redford Naturals to all of her picky eaters! Ellen's grand dog, Marley is also enjoying the Redford Naturals Chicken and Brown Rice Senior Diet. As you can see, we all care because we share in the concern for the wellbeing and health of every pet! Come visit us today!

Mission Statement

Our mission is to provide our customers with a great selection of high quality Pet Products ,Services ,and Pet Foods at the best price possible. We invite you to shop and compare our prices with any other pet supply store, pet supply chain store, grocery store or mass market retailer. We have everyday low prices and hot weekly specials!

Certified Pet Dietitian & Pet Nutrition Specialists

In today's market it is difficult to sift through the various opinions out there as to what is appropriate to feed your dogs and cats. In order to assist consumers navigate these tumultuous times, our staff has undergone professional training in Pet Nutrition and Raw Food feeding and earned their Pet Nutrition Specialist status. Some of our staff has also enrolled in or completed a Certified Pet Dietitian program, the first of its kind! Stop wondering what advice is the right advice and talk to our certified and trained staff.
